ABOUT US

The Villa Collective was born out of a lifetime of experience.

WHO WE ARE

Villa Collective is an independent, villa rental company specialising in high end villas for discerning clients. The founder, Nick Cookson, is based between his London and Greek homes where he heads up a small, knowledgeable team focussed on excellent, personal service. Nick inherited his passion for Greece and a love of this specialist niche of the travel industry from his family, who built the pioneering luxury villa company, Corfu Villas (now CV Villas), in the 70’s.

WHAT WE DO

We source and inspect the best rental properties in the Mediterranean through an extensive network of contacts. While our main focus is on villas in Greece, we have an expanding portfolio across Europe and Morocco where the same principles apply.

Our team works closely with each client to understand their exacting requirements and recommend suitable villas. We continue to liaise with each client in the lead up to their holidays and cooperate with our specialist suppliers to help plan bespoke services. Transfers, car hire, boat hire, crewed yachts and private chefs are organised regularly in addition to the villa rental, with optional extras such as spa treatments and shopping services too.

HOW WE DO IT DIFFERENTLY

Firstly, we are extremely selective in the kind of villas we agree to include in our portfolio. Most are unique, private holiday homes, built with love by owners as places to live. An exceptional location, high quality furnishings and excellent staff are priorities.

Secondly, we only work with villas where we have direct contact with their owners and managers with whom we build relationships through transparent communication. Being a boutique operation means personal service goes beyond what most larger villa companies offer. We also provide a more thorough trip planning service than most of our smaller competitors.
